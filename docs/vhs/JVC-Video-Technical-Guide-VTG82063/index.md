# JVC Video Technical Guide VTG82063

This guide is split into eight sections from the original JVC Video Technical Guide VTG82063. The image and figure assets are kept in the shared `assets` folder for the guide.

## Sections

- [Section 1 - VHS Basics Technology](section-1.md): VHS and S-VHS format fundamentals, cassette construction, tape paths, recording patterns, format compatibility, and core terminology.
- [Section 2 - Mechanism Description](section-2.md): Mechanical deck construction, loading and unloading operation, tape transport, mode transitions, and mechanism control details.
- [Section 3 - Video Circuit](section-3.md): Luminance and chrominance signal processing, recording and playback paths, S-VHS processing, timebase correction topics, and video circuit blocks.
- [Section 4 - Servo Circuit](section-4.md): Drum and capstan servo principles, motor drive circuits, tracking control, sensors, phase control, and related timing tables.
- [Section 5 - Microprocessor Systems](section-5.md): System control architecture, timer and mechanism CPUs, serial communication, key input handling, display control, and mode management.
- [Section 6 - Tuner Circuit](section-6.md): NTSC, PAL, and SECAM tuner/IF signal paths, channel reception, synthesizer tuning, TV audio multiplex systems, NICAM, and VPS material.
- [Section 7 - Audio Circuit](section-7.md): Fixed-head audio, VHS Hi-Fi depth-multiplex recording, FM audio recording/playback flow, noise reduction, and audio circuit behavior.
- [Section 8 - Appendix](section-8.md): Supplemental reference material, abbreviations, glossary entries, and supporting technical definitions.
